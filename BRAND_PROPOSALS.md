## Strategic Recommendation

### 1. The SEO Powerhouse: `ZeroUpload.io` or `NoCloudConvert.com`
If the primary goal is to capture search traffic from people worried about privacy (e.g., "convert HEIC to PNG without uploading"), these names act as their own marketing copy.

### 2. The Modern Utility: `LocalConvert.io`
This is the most balanced name. It is professional, describes the "how" (local), and the "what" (convert). It fits the Next.js/WASM tech stack perfectly.

### 3. The Trust Brand: `VaultShift.com` or `PrivaFile.com`
If the long-term plan is to move into premium/enterprise "Secure File Handling," these names build the most trust and authority.
